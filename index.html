How it works

The calendar unlocks when the checklist is done

Step 1

Define your document checklist per appointment type

Set required uploads for each appointment: prior-year return, W-2s, 1099s. You decide what "ready" means.

Step 2

The booking link stays locked until docs are uploaded

Your client sees a portal, not a calendar. Upload each document — only when complete does the slot become bookable.

Step 3

Every appointment starts with everything in hand

No surprises. No chasing. The gate trains the behavior — permanently. Clients who prepare faster finish faster.

TaxDome users

TaxDome lets clients book before they upload anything.

TaxDome has organizers, reminders, and a great client portal. But the appointment slot is still available the moment a client clicks your Calendly link. DocGate adds the gate that TaxDome does not have: no upload, no booking.

Calendly / Acuity users

Your current intake form is optional. This isn't.

Calendly and Acuity let you add intake questions — but clients can skip them and still book. DocGate replaces the optional reminder with an enforced prerequisite. The slot does not appear until every document is in.
